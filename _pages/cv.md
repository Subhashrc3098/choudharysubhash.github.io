Research Experience
======
I work as a Senior Research Fellow at the Indian Institute of Science Education and Research (IISER), Pune, under the supervision of **Dr. Divyang Bhimani**. My research focuses on harmonic analysis and nonlinear evolution equations, particularly the Schrödinger equation.

I have studied Strichartz estimates and their applications to single Schrödinger equations and systems of Schrödinger equations in the Euclidean setting. In joint work with my supervisor, we have established orthonormal Strichartz estimates on the torus as well as in waveguide settings; this work is available as a preprint on arXiv. I have also explored decoupling-type inequalities associated with settings of vanishing curvature.

Publications
======

* **Orthonormal Strichartz estimates on torus and waveguide manifold and applications**, preprint, arXiv.
